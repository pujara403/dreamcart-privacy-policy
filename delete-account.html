Account Deletion Request

DreamCart by Pujara allows users to request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request account deletion, please send an email from your registered email address to:

pujaradreamlife@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Your registered name
  • Your registered phone number
  • A request stating that you want your account deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information (Name, Phone, Address)
  • Order history
  • Uploaded payment screenshots

Data Retention

Some transaction records may be retained for legal or accounting purposes for up to 90 days.
